Flight Simulation

Software-in-the-loop(SITL) was performed for the BWB-UAV using Gazebo simulator.Initially 3D model of the drone was built in the SOLIDWORKS then it was converted into urdf file. Various aerodynamic parameters of BWB (obtained from xflr5), was also integrated to the urdf file. Finally, the mission was planned in QGround Control and simulated flight was observed in Gazebo.
