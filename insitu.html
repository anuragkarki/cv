Flight in-situ Data Acquisition

Research Methodology

    • Various sensors were integrated to arduino to build a DAS.
    • Telemetry was integrated to DAS for communication.
    • Final DAS(Data Acquisation system) model was attached to a quadcopter.
    • Flight was initialized from the remote.
    • Live data collected from sensors were transmitted to groundstation through telemetry.
    • Live data received in ground station was processed to 2d and 3d graph using python.

Conclusion

    • Live environmental data like temperature, humidity etc. were collected and graphed
    • UAV merged with DAS is a stepping stone for decision support mechanism for various missions.
    • Missions like pollution source finding, wildlife conservation requires collection of spontaneous data and use of the data to make decision for the drone.
